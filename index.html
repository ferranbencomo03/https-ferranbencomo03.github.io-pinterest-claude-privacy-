CraftedDocsShop Pin Publisher

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how CraftedDocsShop Pin Publisher ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our application to publish content to Pinterest via the Pinterest API.

1. Information We Access

When you connect the App to your Pinterest account, we may access:

  • Your Pinterest profile information (name, username)
  • Your boards and pins, solely to enable publishing functionality
  • OAuth tokens required to authenticate with the Pinterest API

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information accessed exclusively to:

  • Publish pins to your Pinterest boards on your behalf
  • Display your boards so you can select a publishing destination

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

The App does not permanently store your Pinterest content or personal data on our servers. Authentication tokens are stored locally on your device and are only transmitted to Pinterest's API endpoints.

4. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with:

  • Pinterest API – to publish content. Subject to Pinterest's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic Claude API – to assist with content generation. Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

5. Your Rights

You can revoke the App's access to your Pinterest account at any time via your Pinterest account settings under Apps and websites. This will immediately disconnect the App.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information accessed. All communication with the Pinterest API and Anthropic API is performed over HTTPS.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
